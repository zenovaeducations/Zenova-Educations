OUR DECLARATION

Public money should build public progress — not political wealth.

India does not lack money. India lacks honest systems.

Every year, lakhs of crores are lost through corruption, political greed, scams, bribery and misuse of public money.

That money belongs to the people of India.

It should build: schools, hospitals, roads, jobs, clean water systems and opportunities.

Rebuild India is a youth-led movement created to rebuild India through transparency, development and accountable governance.

We do not join hands with existing political parties.

We stand with the people of India.
